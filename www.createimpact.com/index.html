Mission

The Create Impact's mission is to provide excellent customer services by connecting the business community through variety of solutions and services within affordability and budget

Vision

Our vision is to expand footprint in small and medium enterprises and inspire people and business around the globe through the creative ideas and multicultural experience

Why choose IMPACT?

IMPACT is all about helping you make a positive impression with your customer base. It’s about first impressions--and then lasting impressions. It’s about influencing how the world sees you, positioning you in the best light possible so you can keep doing what it is that you do best.

Responsive

With IMPACT, there is no call center, no check-box formula for designing your project, no waiting in line. Rebecca has owned and operated IMPACT since 2001, and when you reach out, you connect with her directly, every time. If it’s important to you, it’s important to us.

Full Service

A one-stop shop: our creative work includes graphic design, web design, photography, and copy writing. We produce brochures, annual reports, business forms, campaign materials, mailers, posters, websites and more. We can help you with anything in print, keep your website up to date (or build a new one!), and capture great images that you can use for years to come.

Personalised

Your business is unique, and your marketing solutions should be too. IMPACT works with you to create a customized, professional look that will set you apart from your competitors. Just imagine--what if everyone knew how good you are at what you do?
